Welcome To Hotel Karajaw

Welcome to Hotel Karajaw
the haven of your weekend

From its name "Karajaw" which means nice in Cantilangnon, the Hotel Karajaw is the most suitable place to feel comfortable during your visit in Surigao del Sur. It is located at Otriz Street, Magosilom, Cantilan, Surigao del Sur.

The Hotel Karajaw is owned by the members of Cantilan Community Multipurpose Cooperative and managed by the Focus Director Ms. Sheryl Asio. The hotel was constructed in June 2017 and started the operation in August 5, 2017.
